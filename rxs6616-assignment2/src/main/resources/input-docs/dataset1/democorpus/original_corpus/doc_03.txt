Bharat & Dorris Hair And Makeup Awards. Bharat & Dorris Hair and Make-up Awards 2013, were recently held in Mumbai. The show witnessed celebrities like Salman Khan, Hrithik Roshan, Anil Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor, Jackie Shroff, Manish Malhotra and Raveena Tandon presenting awards to their respective hair and make-up artists.
