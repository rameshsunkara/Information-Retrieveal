After two hugely successful films (Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara and Agneepath), 2013 will be a huge year for Hrithik Roshan and his fans around the world. His home-production, the super-ambitious Krrish 3, will release during Diwali 2013. A few other films will also go on floors this year.

Below is a detailed list of Hrithik Roshan’s Upcoming Movies in 2013.

Krrish 3: Along with Yash Raj Films’ Dhoom 3, Krrish 3 is one of the most awaited sequels of 2013. The superhero film will be Hrithik’s solo release this year. Talking to the media on his birthday (January 10th), the actor said “We have been working on Krrish 3 for almost 5 years. Still a long way to go, a lot of special effects are still left. The film has gone beyond our imagination and has been the toughest challenge ever”. Krrish 3 also stars Priyanka Chopra, Vivek Oberoi and Kangna Ranaut. The film is directed by Rakesh Roshan. The Roshan’s wrapped up the film on Rakesh Roshan’s 42nd wedding anniversary. Shahrukh Khan’s Red Chillies Entertainment has been working on the Visual Special Effects. Release Date: 3rd November 2013 (Sunday).

Bang Bang: Director Sidhharth Anand’s next, the official remake of Hollywood film “Knight and Day”, will feature Hrithik Roshan and Katrina Kaif. The film has been tentatively titled ‘Bang Bang’. The shoot was delayed by 3 months, as the Kashmir schedule was moved to December due to security issues. The first shooting schedule now begins in the second week of May. Stunt director of Hollywood films like Rambo has been roped in to choreograph the high-octane action sequences. Release date: 1st May 2014.
