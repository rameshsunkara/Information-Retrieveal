Shuddhi: Karan Johar recently announced Agneepath director Karan Malhotra’s next, which will be produced under the Dharma Productions’ banner. Hrithik Roshan has signed the film. The script is original and NOT adapted from Amish Tripati’s The Immortals of Meluha. Shuddhi releases next Diwali – October 23rd 2014 (date not officially confirmed).

Tigmanshu Dhulia film: After critically acclaimed films like Haasil, Saheb Biwi Aur Gangster and Paan Singh Tomar, director Tigmanshu Dhulia will be directing Hrithik Roshan in the adaptation of Hamlet - William Shakespeare’s most powerful and influential tragedy in English literature. The film will be set in rural India. Shooting begins after the completion of Shuddhi. The untitled film is likely to release in the first half of 2015.
